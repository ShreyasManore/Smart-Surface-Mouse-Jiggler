## Component Selection Notes

- **Motor**: Prioritize gear-reduced motors over high-speed motors; gear reduction provides the low RPM needed without additional speed-control circuitry.
- **Battery**: Use a protected 18650 cell for built-in overcurrent and overvoltage protection.
- **Disc material**: Should be rigid enough to avoid flexing under the mouse's weight, and light enough not to overload the motor.
